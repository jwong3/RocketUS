How To Use This Website

This web application is to help with the safety problem in Worcester. Many people will not go to a location if they are unsure of the safeness of it. When a person knows a location is safe they are more likely to go there. Similarly, if an area feels unsafe then people will be less inclined to use that area.

This application is meant to help people with creating a setting of safety in the community of Worcester. Anyone can add a safeness rating of both the place and surrounding location. To do this, click on add a rating and choose from the drop down box. You can add a location by hitting add. And edit a location by double clicking on a row.




This was created at a Rocket Software Hackathon by Joan Wong and Alexandra Wheeler.
